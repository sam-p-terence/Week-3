This is the neigborhood around me!

In San Francisco we have the Golden Gate Park

To the North, you will reach the Golden Gate Bridge

To the East, you will reach the the metropolis Downtown Financial District as well as Japantown and Chinatown

To the South, you will reach Pacifica

To the West, you will reach Ocean Beach

There are many local restaurants around the area, including Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Ethiopean, Cambodian, French, and lots of local seafood inspired restaurants.

Because the city is located on the water, seafood dishes which include Dungeness crab, oyster, and various types of fish are a large cuisine within the area.
